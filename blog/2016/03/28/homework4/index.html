1（3）

解： $$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} =\frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}-\frac{x\cdot 2x}{2(\sqrt{x^2+y^2})^3} =\frac{y^2}{(\sqrt{x^2+y^2})^3} $$ $$\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} =-\frac{x\cdot 2y}{2(\sqrt{x^2+y^2})^3} =-\frac{xy}{(\sqrt{x^2+y^2})^3} $$ \(\therefore\)函数的全微分为： $$ dz=\frac{y^2}{(\sqrt{x^2+y^2})^3}dx-\frac{xy}{(\sqrt{x^2+y^2})^3}dy $$

3

解：设函数为 \(f(x,y)=\sin{x}\tan{y} \)， $$\frac{\partial f}{\partial x} =\cos{x}\tan{y},\ \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} =\sin{x}\sec ^2{y} $$ 取\(x=30^{\circ},y=45^{\circ},\Delta x=-1^{\circ},\Delta y=1^{\circ} \)，则有 $$\begin{align} \Delta f & \approx df =\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\Delta x+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\Delta y\\ & =\cos{30^{\circ}}\tan{45^{\circ}}(-1^{\circ})+\sin{30^{\circ}}\sec ^2{45^{\circ}}(1^{\circ})\\ & =\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\cdot 1\cdot (-\frac{2\pi}{360})+\frac{1}{2}\cdot(\sqrt{2})^2\cdot(\frac{2\pi}{360})\\ & =\frac{\pi}{180}(1-\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2})\\ & \approx \frac{3.14}{180}(1-\frac{1.732}{2})\\ & \approx 0.0023 \end{align} $$ $$\therefore \sin{29^{\circ}}\tan{46^{\circ}} =\sin{30^{\circ}}\tan{45^{\circ}}+\Delta f \approx 0.5+0.0023 =0.5023 $$
